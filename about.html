The Majlis Hotel

At The Majlis Hotel, we don't just offer a place to stay; we provide an experience that transcends the ordinary. Nestled in Mumbai, our haven is a sanctuary of luxury, where every detail is meticulously crafted to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.

The Majlis Hotel has been a heartthrob of the backpacker community for over 15 years. The Majlis Hotel has established a reputation that redefines the paradigm of excellence in service. They are focused to provide comfortable accommodation with a friendly environment for the guests. The rooms are bestowed with air-conditioning, designed authentically, and newly remodeled for the comfort and pleasure of the travelers.

The spacious, luxurious and elegantly appointed rooms convey a warm and welcoming atmosphere. we understand our guest's needs, therefore let us offer the best quality service to meet higher standards with customer satisfaction.
